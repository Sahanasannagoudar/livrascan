Indigenous Single-Wavelength Platform

LivraScan leverages advanced optical physics to provide laboratory-grade accuracy at the bedside. By analyzing light absorption at an optimized proprietary wavelength, our proprietary algorithm provides precise results by minimizing interference, ensuring reliable data for neonatal samples.

  • Zero Sample Prep: No centrifugation or plasma separation required.
  • Micro-Volume Sampling: Operates on less than 20µL of whole blood.
  • Interference Immunity: Advanced turbidity and hemolysis correction built-in.
  • Rapid Turnaround: Accelerates clinical decision-making during the critical first hours of life.
